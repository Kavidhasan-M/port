Tamilpreneur Fellowship Program 2021

I was selected for the fellowship program conducted by Tamilpreneur. Tamilpreneur is a startup community where they help people who are trying to build startups to connect with investors and mentors. They are building the entrepreneurship ecosystem in Tamilnadu by motivating budding entrepreneurs. This program aims to give exposure and experience in entrepreneurship to the fellows. It was a six-week program where we had different tasks and followed by a masterclass every week. We had tasks like

  • Create and present a report on a startup
  • User Research
  • Brainstorming Sessions
  • Social media and branding strategies

I learnt a lot from those tasks. I was able to meet so many people like me and made connections with them. This also let me know how an early stage startup works. During the masterclasses, we were able to interact with successful entrepreneurs like

  • Suresh Kumar G, Founder and CEO of Pepul
  • Santhosh Muruganandham, Co-Founder of Kolapasi

They shared their entrepreneurial experiences and clarified our doubts.
